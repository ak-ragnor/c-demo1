history

Founded in the year 2007, May Softech is an Information Technology Engineering Services firm headquartered in Chennai-India, partnering with ISEC Inc 
(www.isecinc.com) headquartered in Denver, USA a prime trades contracting company established in 1967.

We build unique designs

We at May Softech cater to requirements of all types of organizations ranging from Architectural firms, Contractors, Sub-Contractors, specialty contractors, lab equipment manufacturers and individual business customers. We have done detailing for a vast range of projects which include universities, offices, hotels & resorts and schools. Our drawings are used by a wide range of people such as Architects, Manufacturers, Installers, Engineers, sub-contractors, and contractors.

May Softech over the years has retained engineering talent from its very first batch of recruitment who have a combined experience of over 40 years in CAD detailing/Engineering (laboratory & woodwork). The team works continuously to improve quality of drawings and strives to achieve flawless deliverables to the clients.
