About Us

Technical Team

May Softech has a strong team consisting of Mechanical/civil engineers in India who are backed by managerial & technical resources from ISEC in the United States. May Softech over the years has retained engineering talent from its very first batch of recruitment who have a combined experience of over 40 years in CAD detailing/Engineering (laboratory & woodwork). 

Our Clients

May Softech has a set of distinguished and reputed clients who are among the top companies in their respective fields. We at May Softech cater to requirements of all types of organizations ranging from Architectural firms, Contractors, Sub-Contractors, specialty contractors, lab equipment manufacturers and individual business customers. 

History

Founded in the year 2007, May Softech is an Information Technology Engineering Services firm headquartered in Chennai-India, partnering with ISEC Inc (www.isecinc.com) headquartered in Denver, USA a prime trades contracting company established in 1967.
